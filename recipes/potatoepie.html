Potato pie

Description

Potato is one of the most versatile ingredients, being able to be a side dish in many recipes, but here it shines as the mai dish.

The potato pie is a simple, easy and fast recipe to make, with little ingredients it is a life savior in those weekends we have know idea what to make for th meals.

Ingredients

  • 6 big Potatoes
  • 2 spoon fulls of butter
  • 300 ml of milk
  • Groundbeef
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the Groundbeef
  2. Cook the Potatoes in boiling water
  3. Drain the water and smash the potatoes
  4. Mix the smashed potatoes with butter and milk
  5. Add half of the dough in the ovenpan, the groundbeef in the middle and the rest of the smashed potatoes on top
  6. Add the cheese on top and put it in the oven until the it is gratined
