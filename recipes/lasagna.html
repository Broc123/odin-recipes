Description

Lasagna is a classic italian dish, combining pasta and diferent types of sauce it has different version worldwide.

This recipe focus at providing a easy way to make lasagna align with a similar italian recipe, avoiding too many changes.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • 1 liter of milk
  • 3 spoon fulls of flour
  • 3 spoon fulls of butter
  • Groundbeef
  • Pasta for lasagna
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Add the butter and flour in a pan
  2. Add the milk and mix it until it thickens
  3. Cook the Groundbeef
  4. Mix the tomato sauce to the cooked Groundbeef
  5. Cook the pasta in boiling water with salt
  6. Add a layer of pasta white sauce and bolognese sauce, repeat it till nothing is left
  7. Add cheese on top and put it in the oven
  8. Take the lasagna when the cheese is gratined
